About Henry

I can remember the first time I realized I was fascinated with technology, when my father and I built my first computer together. We had spent weeks researching, planning and a few hours assembling to see my rag-tag collection of components come together to make a machine that in my mind, could do anything. Ever since then, I've immersed myself in the world of technology by taking roles as an IT intern in stunningly large companies to developing a simple phone app in my bedroom. As somebody who has yet to graduate, I hope to continue my exploration of that world and to become a better consumer and developer to prepare myself for the fast-paced, ever-changing world I will soon be working in.
